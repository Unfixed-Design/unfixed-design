CONTRIBUTE TO THE ARCHIVE /////// This project is concerned with the material inheritances and spatial negotiations that animate Glasgow. By attending to self-built adaptations—such as those of the city’s Showpeople community—and to the lingering logics of industrial infrastructures, the work traces a lived urbanism that persists alongside, and often in spite of, official plans. The material on this site, along with our public-facing events, invites addition, refusal, and re-reading. Rather than settle or conclude, we suggest that Glasgow’s spaces remain in motion: unsettled, provisional, and continually shaped through entanglement.

You're welcome to contribute in whatever form feels right—text, audio, video, or links to your work. Submissions can be anonymous or named. We will be in touch. ///////

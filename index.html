///DESIGN

This is a feedback-driven project focused on overlooked architectural expression. Currently based in Glasgow, the aim is to display how cultural practices, slow-form spatial use and atypical building can form overarching entities of value. In specific circumstances, it is not just what builders build, but how they build that should be seen as forms worthy of conservation. Assessment criteria should account for this. Follow us here as we move along.
